You can retrieve realtime updates for Welcome (camera) by entering a Port and your IP or Domain on which ioBroker is reachable from within the Internet. Netatmo will then push events to that IP/Domain & Port, so that you can receive events of new persons or movement without waiting for the update timer to tick.
